Document Requirements

For new cases you must attach one Case Initiation document. The Civil Case Cover Sheet is recommended but not required. Please click the Cancel Add button to proceed. For subsequent filings you must attach one lead document.

Attaching Document Steps:

1. Select a Category to Identify the document
2. Select document type.
3. Add meta data information for the Court.

  1. Title field is the name of the document that is transmitted to the Court and can be edited.
  2. Depending upon the document type, you may be prompted to provide Additional information that appears after Title.
  3. Depending on which documents you are attaching, an email address may be required for filers.

4. Locate the document by clicking on Browse.
5. Upload the document by clicking on Attach.
